Churros Recipe

About Churros

Cinnamon and sugar-topped fried pastry dough stick. Simlar to doughnuts, and they are fried instead of baked

How to Make Churros

Ingredients

  • Water
  • Sugar
  • Salt
  • Oil
  • Flour
  • Cinnamon

Steps

  1. Boil water, sugar, salt and vegetable oil. Remove heat, and then stir the flour
  2. Transfer dough to pastry bag, and pipe into strips
  3. Fry the strips in hot oil until golden
  4. Drain the churros and roll in cinnamon-sugar
